Dino Ambrosi is a digital wellness advocate and the founder of Project Reboot, an initiative dedicated to helping students develop healthier relationships with technology.

In addition to speaking engagements, Ambrosi collaborates with educational institutions to implement programs that promote digital well-being. His approach focuses on reshaping mindsets, redesigning environments, and establishing healthier tech habits among students.

Opal is a screen time management app designed to help users reduce distractions and enhance focus by limiting access to selected apps and websites.

Key features of Opal include:

Block Sessions: Users can schedule specific times to block selected apps, games, or websites. OPAL

Focus Score®: Opal provides a real-time assessment of a user's focus level throughout the day. OPAL

App Limits and App Lock: These features allow users to set daily usage limits on specific apps and create semi-permanent locks.

One Sec is a screen time management app designed to help users reduce impulsive social media usage by introducing a brief pause before opening selected apps or websites.
